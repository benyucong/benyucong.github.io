Teaching Assistant for Scalable Systems and Data Management D course (2024-2025, 2025-2026 academic years). This course covers the design and implementation of scalable data management systems, including data-center technologies, data models, storage architectures, indexing, query processing, distributed storage, transaction processing, and parallel architectures.
